As you approach the door of strange tower, the door creaks open and a hooded, hunched figure beckons you inside. You follow this strange scientist as he shuffles into a a room cluttered with lab equipment, papers, strange vials, and overturned furniture. A couldron of shimmering purple liquid bubbles next to three enormous tanks that contains the most monstrous creatures you have ever seen.

"Are you ready?" Giggles the scientist as he bounces on the balls of his feet in excitement, "My children are most eager to play!"
