THE LABORATORY

As you approach the strange tower, the grand wooden flings open to reveal Tim Curry in a glittering bodice with matching gloves, an elegant pearl necklace, and a wicked glint in his dramatically-contoured eyes.

...purrs the scientist as he beckons you inside. As soon as you take a tentative step inside, he grabs you and drags you towards the wire cage of a shaky-looking elevator.

He struts into the elevator and whips around, hand outstretched towards you. "My beautiful abominations are just DYING to get their teeth on a new toy...

Will you come play with my darlings?"
